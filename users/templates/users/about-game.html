Doors of drums

The game has the purpose of teaching everyone who is interested in the world of percussion about the different instruments that exist. The game takes place in the XXI century, where we will accompany a child who wants to know various percussive instruments because since childhood he admired how his father played the timpani. The user goes through the most important stages of his life in his path of learning percussions, discovering various instruments along the way and of course, opening the doors to new opportunities in his life.
